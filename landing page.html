International Yoga Day

Every year on June 21, the world unites to celebrate International Yoga Day, a day dedicated to raising awareness about the ancient Indian practice of yoga and its manifold benefits for physical and mental well-being.

The theme of International Yoga Day 2024 is “Yoga for Women Empowerment,” focusing on enhancing the physical and mental well-being of women. To celebrate, various events and sessions will be organized globally to promote the practice of yoga.
